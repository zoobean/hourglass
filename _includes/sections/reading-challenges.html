Access Reading Challenges to Stay Motivated

Time-bound and themed reading challenges are fun for kids and typically motivate them to read over 20% more each day. The Hourglass gives families free access to local, national, and school-based reading challenges through the Beanstack app. These challenges may reward individual kids with prizes or give them a way to contribute to a good cause.
